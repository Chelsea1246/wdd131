YOUTH EMPOWERMENT & SKILLS DEVELOPMENT HUB

Website Planning Document

Site Name

YOUTH EMPOWERMENT & SKILLS DEVELOPMENT HUB

The website will provide information on youth skills development, career guidance, internship opportunities, scholarships, and personal growth resources. It will also include articles, training opportunities, and success stories aimed at helping young people prepare for education, employment, and leadership roles.t

Optional domain name: youthskillshub.com

Site Purpose

Purpose of the Website The purpose of the website is to empower young people by providing them with the resources and information they need to develop their skills, find career opportunities, and achieve personal growth. The website will serve as a comprehensive hub for youth empowerment and skill development.

Scenarios

  • What skills or training programs can help me improve my employability?
  • Where can I find internship opportunities in my field of interest?
  • What scholarships are available for students pursuing higher education?

Color Schema

  • Green (#2f855a): Used for headings and accents.
  • Light Gray (#edf2f7): Used for the page background.

Typography

  • Poppins: Used for headings and body text for readability.
